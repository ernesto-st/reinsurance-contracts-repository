“Act of Terrorism” as used herein shall follow the definition provided under the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002 (TRIA) and as amended by the Terrorism Risk Insurance Extension Act of 2005 (TRIEA) and the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program Reauthorization Acts of 2007, 2015, and 2019 (TRIPRA), together and including any extensions, reauthorizations, or replacement thereof, the “Terrorism Act.”
