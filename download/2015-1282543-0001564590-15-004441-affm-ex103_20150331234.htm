A.

IT IS HEREBY AGREED, effective March 31, 2015, that this Endorsement deletes and replaces in its entirety the Commutation and Release Agreement previously signed by the Company and the Subscribing Reinsurer on March 6, 2015.

B.

IT IS FURTHER AGREED, effective March 31, 2015, that the Company and the Subscribing Reinsurer, in order to fully and finally settle and commute all obligations and liabilities known and unknown under this Contract, but only as respects losses commencing during the period from June 30, 2014 through March 31, 2015, agree to the following:

1.

Subject to the terms and conditions of this Endorsement, the Company accepts payment of $22,212,002 (the “Commutation Amount “) from the Subscribing Reinsurer in full and final settlement of any and all amounts due from the Company to the Reinsurer under this Contract (or portion thereof) and under the Automobile Quota Share Reinsurance Contract, effective December 31, 2013 as specified in Endorsement No. 4, effective March 31, 2015 ,attached to the Subscribing Reinsurer’s Interests and Liabilities Agreement pertaining thereto.
