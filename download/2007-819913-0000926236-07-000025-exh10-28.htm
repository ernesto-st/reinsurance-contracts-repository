AMENDMENT 3
QUOTA SHARE REINSURANCE TREATY
ATTACHING JANUARY 1, 2006
BY AND BETWEEN
AMERICAN HALLMARK INSURANCE COMPANY (AHIC) AND
PHOENIX INDEMNITY INSURANCE COMPANY (PIIC) AND
GULF STATES INSURANCE COMPANY (GSIC)

 

The parties hereby agree to amend ARTICLE 2 - SUBJECT BUSINESS of the quota share reinsurance treaty as follows:
ARTICLE 2 - SUBJECT BUSINESS
     This agreement shall apply to all insurance business in force of AHIC, PIIC, and GSIC at the Attachment Date and all new and renewal insurance business written thereafter by AHIC, PIIC, and GSIC (the “Subject Business”) including but not limited to business written or renewed in the State of Florida, provided however, it shall only apply to business in the state of Florida that is written or renewed on or after Dec 18, 2006.
