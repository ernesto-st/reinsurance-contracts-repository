WHEREAS, the Borrower and each of its Subsidiaries are party to that certain Amended and Restated Credit Agreement, dated as of April 25, 2002, as amended from time to time thereafter (as amended, the “Existing Credit Agreement”), among the Borrower, each of its Subsidiaries, the lenders party thereto, and Bank of America, N.A., as the Agent for the Lenders; and

WHEREAS, the Borrower and the Guarantors have requested, and the Lenders have agreed to provide the requested $390,000,000 five-year revolving credit facility (the “Credit Facility”) available to the Borrower on the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth, which will be used in part to repay all amounts outstanding under the Existing Credit Agreement.
