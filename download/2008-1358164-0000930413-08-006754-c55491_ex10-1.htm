                This letter confirms the arrangements under which J.P. Morgan Securities Inc. (“JPMorgan”) is engaged by Weil, Gotshal & Manges LLP (“WGM”) to assist WGM in its representation of Syncora Guarantee Inc. and its subsidiaries and affiliates (collectively, “Syncora”), in identifying and analyzing potential strategic alternatives with respect to Syncora’s portfolio of credit default swap and financial guarantee contracts (collectively, the “Contracts”) identified in Appendix 1a, Appendix 1b and Appendix 1c, respectively (together, the “Portfolio”), including one or more Transactions (as defined below).
