WHEREAS, Borrower and Lender desire to amend and restate the Original Loan Agreement to govern the terms of the Original Loan and the Additional Loan.
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the making of the Original Loan and the Additional Loan by Lender and the covenants, agreements, representations and warranties set forth in this Agreement, Borrower and Lender, hereby amend and restate the Original Loan Agreement in all respects and from and after the date hereof the Loan consisting of the Original Loan and the Additional Loan shall be governed by the terms of this Agreement:
