3.2 Title and Survey Objections. Purchaser may advise Seller in writing and in reasonable detail, not later than 10 days prior to the expiration of the Due Diligence Period, what exceptions or survey irregularities, if any, are not acceptable to Purchaser (the “Title and Survey Objections”). 3.3 Removal of Liens; Affidavits.
