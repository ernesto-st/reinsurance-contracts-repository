THIS AMENDED AND RESTATED MASTER LOAN AGREEMENT (this “Agreement”) is dated to be effective as of June 29, 2017, by and between HOME FEDERAL SAVINGS BANK, a federally chartered stock savings bank organized under the laws of the United States (“Lender”) and HOMELAND ENERGY SOLUTIONS, LLC, an Iowa limited liability company (“Borrower”).
RECITALS

A.    Borrower has requested Lender extend to it various credit facilities for the purposes of refinancing certain existing indebtedness and to finance certain expansion projects associated with its ethanol production facility located near the city of New Hampton, Chickasaw County, Iowa.

B.    Borrower had previously borrowed certain funds from Lender under that certain Master Loan Agreement dated as of November 30, 2007 (the “Original Loan Agreement”) and related supplements and promissory notes for the purpose of acquiring and constructing the aforementioned ethanol production facility; and this Agreement is meant to amend and restate the Original Loan Agreement but is not a novation thereof.

C.    Lender has agreed to make such loans to Borrower, upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth herein.
