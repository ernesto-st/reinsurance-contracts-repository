SCHEDULE X
(Release Price Calculation Example)
Assume:
1. The total prior Released Allocated Loan Amounts as of the anticipated date of the Property Sale proposed in a Sale Request is $10,000,000;
2. The Allocated Loan Amount for the Property that is the subject of the proposed Property Sale is $1,000,000; and
3. The Release Price Threshold Amount is $10,325,000.
Calculation:
The $1,000,000 Allocated Loan Amount for the Property that is the subject of the proposed Property Sale would be split so that:
(a) $325,000 would be multiplied by 115% (which equals $373,750); and
(b) The remaining balance of $675,000 would be multiplied by 120% (which equals $810,000)
Therefore, the Release Price would equal $1,183,750.
