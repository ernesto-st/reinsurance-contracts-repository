WHEREAS, the parties hereto are party to the Amended and Restated RFC Loan Agreement, dated as of October 2, 2002 (the “Existing Loan Agreement”), among the Company, RFC, the Banks and the Agent, pursuant to which the Company could request from RFC, and RFC could, in its sole discretion, agree to make to the Company, revolving loans;
