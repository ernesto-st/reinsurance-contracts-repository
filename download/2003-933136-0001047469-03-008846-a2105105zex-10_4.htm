Litigation

        Several of Finance's subsidiaries and their current and former employees are defendants in a number of suits pending in the state and federal courts of Mississippi. In one of the pending cases, Carolyn Baker, et al. v. Washington Mutual Finance Group, LLC f/k/a City Finance Company, a jury awarded just over $71 million against one of Finance's subsidiaries, Washington Mutual Finance Group, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("WMF Group"). Pursuant to a motion filed by WMF Group, the trial court reduced the verdict to just over $53 million.
