RECITALS
     Purchaser’s primary business is residential homebuilding in the Washington, D.C. and Raleigh, North Carolina metropolitan areas. The Company is similarly engaged in the business of residential homebuilding in the metropolitan Atlanta, Georgia area, with additional operations in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina and Charlotte, North Carolina.
     The parties hereto have determined that it is in their best interests for Purchaser to acquire all of the Shares. The Sellers have therefore agreed to sell, and Purchaser has agreed to purchase, all such Shares on the terms, and subject to the conditions, contained in this Agreement.
