LOAN AND SECURITY AGREEMENT

THIS LOAN AND SECURITY AGREEMENT (this “Agreement”) dated as of July 11, 2006, by Alexion Manufacturing LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“Borrower”), having an address at c/o Alexion Pharmaceuticals Inc., 352 Knotter Drive, Cheshire, Connecticut 06410, and iSTAR FINANCIAL INC., a Maryland corporation (together with its successors and assigns, hereinafter referred to as “Lender”), with offices at 1114 Avenue of the Americas, 27th Floor, New York, New York 10036.
