  A. This Contract shall take effect on March 31, 2013, applying to losses occurring on or after that date and shall remain in force for an indefinite period but may be terminated on January 1, 2014, by either party giving to the other party 30 days’ prior written notice, or any January 1 thereafter, by either party giving to the other party 75 days’ prior written notice.

All other terms and conditions of the Contract shall remain unchanged.
