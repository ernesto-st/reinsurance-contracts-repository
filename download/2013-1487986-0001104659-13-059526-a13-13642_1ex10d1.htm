AMENDMENT NO. 1 TO CREDIT AGREEMENT

 

This AMENDMENT NO. 1 TO CREDIT AGREEMENT (this “Amendment”), dated as of June 6, 2013 is entered into by and among HAWAIIAN TELCOM COMMUNICATIONS, INC., a Delaware corporation (the “Borrower”), HAWAIIAN TELCOM HOLDCO, INC., a Delaware corporation (“Holdings”), each of the Lenders listed on the signature pages hereto, CREDIT SUISSE AG, CAYMAN ISLANDS BRANCH, as administrative agent and collateral agent for the Lenders (in such capacity, the “Administrative Agent”).  Capitalized terms used herein and not otherwise defined shall have the meaning assigned to such term in the Amended Credit Agreement (defined below).

 

RECITALS:

 

WHEREAS, the Borrower, Holdings, the Administrative Agent, the Collateral Agent and the other parties listed on the signature pages thereto are parties to that certain Credit Agreement dated as of February 29, 2012 (the “Credit Agreement”; the Credit Agreement, as amended by this Amendment, the “Amended Credit Agreement”);

 

WHEREAS, Credit Suisse Securities (USA) LLC (“CS Securities”) has agreed to act as the sole lead arranger and sole bookrunner (in such capacities, the “Arranger”) with respect to this Amendment, pursuant to that certain Engagement Letter, dated as of May 13, 2013 (the “Engagement Letter”), between the Borrower, Holdings and CS Securities;

 

WHEREAS, with respect to the Lenders holding any Loans existing immediately prior to the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date under the Credit Agreement (such Loans, the “Existing Loans”) whose executed counterpart of this Amendment has not been received by the Administrative Agent on or prior to a deadline (the “Non-Consenting Lenders”; the Lenders that are not the Non-Consenting Lenders are hereinafter referred to as the “Consenting Lenders”) as announced by the Arranger to the Lenders (which may, in the discretion of the Arranger, be extended), which shall be at a time on or about May 20, 2013, 5 P.M., Eastern time (the “Consent Deadline”), the Borrower hereby requires, pursuant to, and subject to the limitations in, Section 2.21(a)(iv) of the Credit Agreement, that each such Non-Consenting Lender assign and delegate all of its interests, rights and obligations under the Credit Agreement and each of the other Loan Documents, including, without limitation, such Non-Consenting Lender’s Existing Loans, to Eligible Assignees party hereto (the “New Lenders”) that have agreed to assume such interests, rights and obligations in principal amounts set forth on their respective signature page hereto (or such lesser amount as shall be allocated by the Arranger);

 

WHEREAS, the Borrower has requested that additional Term Loans (the “Amendment No. 1 Loans”) in an aggregate principal amount of approximately $2.888 million which, together with the principal amount of the other Term Loans would amount to $300 million, be made on the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date for the purpose of financing the call premium payable in connection with the transaction contemplated hereby (the “Call Premium”), and one or more Consenting Lenders or New Lenders (the “Amendment No. 1 Lenders”) are willing to provide such additional Term Loans in principal amounts equal to their respective commitments allocated by the Arranger (the “Amendment No. 1 Commitments”); and

 

WHEREAS, the Borrower has further requested, and the Lenders party hereto have agreed, upon the terms and conditions set forth herein, to enter into this Amendment to effect other amendments to the Credit Agreement and the Guarantee and Collateral Agreement, in each case, as hereinafter set forth;

 


 

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the premises and the agreements, provisions and covenants herein contained, the parties hereto agree as follows:

 

SECTION 1.                         AMENDMENTS TO CREDIT AGREEMENT

 

Subject to the satisfaction of the conditions set forth in Section 4 and effective as of the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date, the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to delete the stricken text (indicated textually in the same manner as the following example: ) and to add the double-underlined text (indicated textually in the same manner as the following example: double-underlined text) as set forth in the pages of the Credit Agreement attached as Exhibit A hereto.  Effective as of the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date, the Consenting Lenders hereby waive any breakage cost or expense payable to them under Section 2.16 of the Credit Agreement that arises from the repricing of the Loans contemplated by this Amendment.

 

SECTION 2.                         AMENDMENTS TO GUARANTEE AND COLLATERAL AGREEMENT

 

Subject to the satisfaction of the conditions set forth in Section 4 and effective as of the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date, the Guarantee and Collateral Agreement is hereby amended:

 

(a)                                 The definition of “Obligations” in Section 1.02 of the Guarantee and Collateral Agreement is hereby amended by inserting the text “(excluding any Excluded Swap Obligations)” immediately prior to semi-colon appearing therein.

 

(b)                                 The following text shall be inserted as a new Section 2.09 to the Guarantee and Collateral Agreement:

 

“Section 2.09 Keepwell. Each Qualified ECP Guarantor hereby jointly and severally absolutely, unconditionally and irrevocably undertakes to provide such funds or other support as may be needed from time to time by each other Loan Party to honor all of its obligations under this Agreement in respect of Swap Obligations (provided, however, that each Qualified ECP Guarantor shall only be liable under this Section 2.09 for the maximum amount of such liability that can be hereby incurred without rendering its obligations under this Section 2.09 , or otherwise under this Agreement, as it relates to such other Guarantor, voidable under applicable law relating to fraudulent conveyance or fraudulent transfer, and not for any greater amount). The obligations of each Qualified ECP Guarantor under this Section 2.09 shall remain in full force and effect with respect to such Qualified ECP Guarantor until the termination of this Agreement or the release of such Guarantor in accordance with the terms of this Agreement. Each Qualified ECP Guarantor intends that this Section 2.09 constitute, and this Section 2.09 shall be deemed to constitute, a “keepwell, support, or other agreement” for the benefit of each other Loan Party for all purposes of Section 1a(18)(A)(v)(II) of the Commodity Exchange Act.”

 

(c)                                  The following text shall be inserted as a new Section 2.10 to the Guarantee and Collateral Agreement:

 

“Section 2.10 Excluded Swap Transactions. Notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained in this Agreement or any provision of any other Loan Document, the Obligations guaranteed hereunder by any Guarantor shall not include obligations in respect of any Excluded Swap Transaction (as defined below) with respect to that Guarantor. For purposes hereof, “Excluded Swap Transaction” means, with respect to any Guarantor, any transaction that constitutes a “swap” within the meaning of section 1a(47) of the Commodity Exchange Act (“Swap”), if, and to the extent that, (i) such Guarantor is not an “eligible contract participant” under and as defined in Section 1a(18)(A)(v) of the Commodity Exchange Act (or CFTC rules, regulations or orders promulgated thereunder (or the application or official interpretation of any thereof)) on the date such

The liability of a Guarantor for any amount payable under the netting provisions of Section 6(e) of an ISDA Master Agreement with respect to any “Terminated Transactions” under and as defined therein shall exclude amounts attributable to Excluded Swap Transactions with respect to such Guarantor.”

 

SECTION 3.                         REPLACEMENT OF NON-CONSENTING LENDERS; ASSIGNMENTS OF CERTAIN LENDERS

 

Subject to the satisfaction of the conditions set forth in Section 4 and effective as of the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date:

 

(a)                                 each Non-Consenting Lender shall, pursuant to Section 2.21(a)(iv) of the Credit Agreement, be replaced and all of its Existing Loans and other interests, rights and obligations under the Credit Agreement shall be transferred and assigned to a New Lender (as allocated by the Arranger), in each case upon the execution and delivery by such New Lender of this Amendment, such that immediately after giving effect to this Amendment on the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date, the amounts of Loans held by each New Lender with respect to such Non-Consenting Lender’s Loans and other interests, rights and obligations under the Credit Agreement are the amounts allocated thereto by the Arranger;

 

(b)                                 the execution and delivery hereof by each New Lender shall also be deemed to be its execution and delivery of an Assignment and Acceptance in the form of Exhibit B to the Credit Agreement as an “Assignee” as defined therein and thereunder, agreeing in such capacity to all the terms therein applicable to it to the extent necessary to reflect the transfer and assignments described in immediately preceding Section 3(a) and the execution and delivery hereof by the Administrative Agent shall be deemed to be an exercise of the irrevocable power of attorney (as set forth in the final sentence of Section 2.21(a) of the Credit Agreement), on behalf of each Non-Consenting Lender as assignor, to effectuate all such transfers and assignments and the Borrower hereby consents to all such transfers and assignments;

 

(c)                                  upon its receipt of the principal amount of its Existing Loans (and other amounts owing in respect thereof) on the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date, each Consenting Lender electing “Consent and Assignments (to be settled after closing)” on its signature hereto shall be deemed to have transferred and assigned all of its Existing Loans and other interests, rights and obligations in respect thereof to the Arranger (or an Affiliate thereof or one or more Eligible Assignees designated by the Arranger) on the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date (the execution and delivery hereof by each such Lender shall also be deemed to be its execution and delivery of an Assignment and Acceptance in the form of Exhibit B to the Credit Agreement as an “Assignor” as defined therein and thereunder, agreeing in such capacity to all the terms therein applicable to it to the extent necessary to reflect the transfer and assignments described herein); and

 

(d)                                 if any transaction contemplated by this Section 3 shall contradict, or is inconsistent with, Sections 2.21 or 9.04 of the Credit Agreement, the Lenders constituting the Required Lenders hereby consent to any such contradiction or inconsistency.

 

Notwithstanding anything to the contrary herein, this Section 3 shall be deemed to be effective immediately prior to the effectiveness of Sections 1 and 2 of this Amendment.

SECTION 4.                         CONDITIONS PRECEDENT TO EFFECTIVENESS

 

The provisions set forth in Sections 1, 2  and 3 hereof shall be effective as of the date first above written (the “Amendment No. 1 Effective Date”) when each of the following conditions shall have been satisfied:

 

(a)                                 Consents.  The Administrative Agent shall have received executed signature pages hereto from (x) Consenting Lenders constituting at least the Required Lenders, (y) New Lenders (which, in the aggregate, shall have set forth principal amounts of their respective signature pages hereto at least equal to the aggregate principal amount of Existing Loans of Non-Consenting Lenders) and (z) each Loan Party.

 

(b)                                 Governmental Approval. All requisite Governmental Authorities and third parties (including, without limitation, the administrative agent for the lenders under the Revolving Facility Agreement pursuant to Section 2(f) of the Intercreditor Agreement) shall have approved or consented to this Amendment and the other transactions contemplated hereby to the extent required, all applicable appeal periods shall have expired and there shall not be any pending or threatened litigation, governmental, administrative or judicial action that could reasonably be expected to restrain, prevent or impose burdensome conditions on this Amendment or the other transactions contemplated hereby.

 

(c)                                  Commitments. Each of the Administrative Agent and the Arranger shall have received commitments from New Lenders that are Eligible Assignees in an amount that, when added to the aggregate outstanding principal amount of Existing Loans of Consenting Lenders, equals at least the aggregate outstanding principal amount of Existing Loans on the Closing Date (as determined immediately before giving effect to this Amendment).

 

(d)                                 Expenses and Fees.  All fees and out-of-pocket costs and expenses owing to the Arranger incurred in connection with the transactions contemplated under this Amendment, as agreed between the Arranger, Holdings and the Borrower in the Engagement Letter, shall have been paid.  All out-of-pocket costs and expenses owing to the Administrative Agent and their Affiliates (including the reasonable fees and out-of-pocket costs and expenses of legal counsel to the Administrative Agent) incurred in connection with the transactions contemplated under this Amendment that are required to be paid pursuant to Section 9.05(a) of the Credit Agreement shall have been paid.  With respect to each Consenting Lender and New Lender whose executed counterpart of this Amendment has been received by the Administrative Agent on or prior to the Consent Deadline, a fee equal to 0.50% of the Existing Loans and the Amendment No. 1 Loans of each such Consenting Lender and New Lender as of the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date shall have been paid by the Borrower.

 

(e)                                  Representations and Warranties.  The representations and warranties set forth in Section 5 shall be true and correct on and as of the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date.

 

(f)                                   No Default or Event of Default.  On and as of the Amendment No. 1 Effective Date and after giving effect to the amendments contemplated herein, no Default or Event of Default shall have occurred and be continuing.

 

(g)                                  Payoff — Non-Consenting Lenders.  The Administrative Agent shall have received evidence that (i) with respect to each Lender, all accrued interest on its Existing Loans under the Credit Agreement, and the Call Premium equal to 1% of the principal amount of each Lender’s Existing Loans,  shall have been paid and (ii) with respect to each Non-Consenting Lender, evidence that all principal and any other amount due and payable to such Non-Consenting Lender under the Credit Agreement in respect of its Existing Loans shall have been paid in full.
