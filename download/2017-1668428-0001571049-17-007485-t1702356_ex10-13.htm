It is agreed that (a) each of RBCCM and MLPFS will act as joint lead arrangers (in such capacity, the “Lead Arrangers”) and as a joint bookrunners for the Senior Bridge Facility; and (b) RBCCM will act as administrative agent for the Senior Bridge Facility (the “Bridge Administrative Agent” or the “Administrative Agent”).
