(State Auto Mutual, State Auto P&C, Milbank, SA WI, Farmers Casualty, SA OH, SA FL, Meridian Security, Meridian Citizens Mutual, Patrons, Litchfield and Beacon are herein collectively referred to as the “Pooled Companies” or “State Auto” and individually as a “Pooled Company”).

Background Information

The Pooled Companies have determined that their business operations should be conducted by employees of State Auto P&C on behalf of each of the Pooled Companies and that State Auto Mutual should remain as the agent for each of the Pooled Companies with respect to collecting and disbursing funds as required by the Pooled Companies’ business operations. These arrangements have been effected through the Management and Operations Agreement Amended and Restated as of January 1, 2005 (the “2005 Management Agreement”), as amended from time to time, as to SA WI through the Amended and Restated Management Agreement dated effective January 1, 2000 (the “2000 Management Agreement”), and by means of mutual reinsurance on a percentage basis as herein provided.

As to Patrons and Litchfield, it is anticipated that their business operations and employees will become fully integrated with the other Pooled Companies on or about January 1, 2009. Until such time, certain management and other services will be made available to Patrons and Litchfield pursuant to the First Amendment to Management Services Agreement (State Auto Mutual, State Auto P&C, Patrons and Litchfield as parties) and Second Amendment to Inter-Company Expense Agreement (State Auto Mutual, State Auto P&C, Patrons and Litchfield as parties) effective January 1, 2008, and by means of mutual reinsurance on a percentage basis as herein provided.
