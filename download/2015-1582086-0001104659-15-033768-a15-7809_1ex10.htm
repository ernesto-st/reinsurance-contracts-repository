FIRST AMENDMENT TO CREDIT AGREEMENT

 

FIRST AMENDMENT TO CREDIT AGREEMENT (this “First Amendment”), entered into as of May 1, 2015, among BLUE CAPITAL REINSURANCE HOLDINGS LTD., a Bermuda exempted limited liability company (“BCR Holdings” or the “Borrower”), MONTPELIER RE HOLDINGS LTD., a Bermuda exempted limited liability company (“Holdings”), the Lenders party hereto and ROYAL BANK OF CANADA, as administrative agent for the Lenders (the “Administrative Agent”).

 

RECITALS:

 

A.                                    The Borrower, Holdings, the Lenders and the Administrative Agent are parties to that certain Credit Agreement dated as of May 2, 2014 (as the same may be amended, restated, modified or supplemented from time to time, the “Credit Agreement”).  Each capitalized term used but not defined herein has the meaning assigned to such term in the Credit Agreement (where the context so requires, after giving effect to this First Amendment).

 

B.                                    The Borrower has requested that the Lenders amend certain provisions of the Credit Agreement as provided for herein.

 

C.                                    The Borrower, Holdings, the Lenders and the Administrative Agent have agreed, on the terms and subject to the conditions contained herein, to amend the Credit Agreement as provided for herein.

 

NOW, THEREFORE, for good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the parties hereto agree as follows:

 

Section 1.                                           Amendments to the Credit Agreement.  Effective as of the First Amendment Effective Date (as defined below), the Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows:

 

(a)                                 Section 1.01 of the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to add the following definitions in the appropriate alphabetical order:

 

“First Amendment” means the First Amendment to Credit Agreement, dated as of May 1, 2015, among the Borrower, Holdings, the Lenders party thereto and the Administrative Agent.

 

“First Amendment Effective Date” has the meaning specified in the First Amendment.

 

“Merger” means the merger of Holdings with and into Merger Sub, with Merger Sub surviving such merger as a wholly owned subsidiary of Parent.

 

“Merger Agreement” means the Agreement and Plan of Merger dated as of March 31, 2015, among Holdings, Parent and Merger Sub.

 


 

“Merger Conditions” has the meaning specified in the First Amendment.

 

“Merger Conditions Effective Date” has the meaning specified in the First Amendment.

 

“Merger Sub” means Millhill Holdings Ltd., a Bermuda exempted limited liability company and a direct, wholly owned subsidiary of Parent.

 

“Parent” means Endurance Specialty Holdings Ltd., a Bermuda exempted limited liability company.

 

(b)                                 The definition of the term “Commitment Termination Date” in Section 1.01 of the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to read in its entirety as follows:

 

“Commitment Termination Date” means the earliest to occur of (i) the date that is three hundred sixty four (364) days after the First Amendment Effective Date, (ii) the date the Revolving Commitments are permanently reduced to zero pursuant to Section 2.05, (iii) the date of the termination of the Revolving Commitments pursuant to Section 8.02 and (iv) the occurrence of a Management Event.

 

(c)                                  The definition of the term “Change of Control” in Section 1.01 of the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to add a new sentence at the end of such definition as follows:

 

Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Merger shall not constitute a Change of Control, provided that the Merger Conditions shall have been satisfied or waived in accordance with Section 10.01 prior to or substantially concurrently with the consummation of the Merger.

 

(d)                                 Section 7.07 of the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to delete the text “and” at the end of clause (e) thereof, replace the text “.” at the end of clause (f) thereof with the text “; and” and add a new clause (g) at the end thereof as follows:

 

(g)                                  Holdings may merge with Merger Sub pursuant to the Merger, with Merger Sub surviving the Merger as a wholly owned subsidiary of Parent, provided that the Merger Conditions shall have been satisfied or waived in accordance with Section 10.01 prior to or substantially concurrently with the consummation of the Merger.

 

(e)                                  Section 7.11 of the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to add a new sentence at the end of such Section:

Section 2.                                           Additional Amendments to the Credit Agreement.  Effective as of the Merger Conditions Effective Date (as defined below), (a) the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to read in its entirety as Exhibit A to this First Amendment and (b) Exhibit A to the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to read in its entirety as Exhibit B to this First Amendment (such amendments, the “Additional Amendments”).
