AMENDED AND RESTATED CREDIT AGREEMENT

        This AMENDED AND RESTATED CREDIT AGREEMENT is entered into as of September 30, 2002, between ZENITH NATIONAL INSURANCE CORP., a Delaware corporation (the "Company") and Bank of America, N.A. (together with its successors and assigns, the "Bank").

        WHEREAS, the Company has entered into the Credit Agreement dated as of July 24, 1997, as amended by the First Amendment to Credit Agreement dated as of January 21, 1998, the Second Amendment to Credit Agreement dated as of July 23, 1998, the Third Amendment to Credit Agreement dated as of August 21, 1998, the Fourth Amendment to Credit Agreement dated as of July 22, 1999, the Fifth Amendment to Credit Agreement dated as of August 9, 1999, the Sixth Amendment to Credit Agreement dated as of July 18, 2000, the Seventh Amendment to Credit Agreement dated as of September 9, 2000, the Eighth Amendment to Credit Agreement dated as of September 18, 2001, and the Ninth Amendment to Credit Agreement dated as of July 24, 2002, (such Credit Agreement, as so amended, the "Existing Agreement") with the Bank, providing for a revolving credit facility; and

        WHEREAS, subject to the terms and conditions set forth below, the Company and the Bank desire to entirely amend and restate, but not to novate, the Existing Agreement.

        NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual agreements, provisions and covenants contained herein, the parties hereto agree that the Existing Agreement is amended and restated in its entirety as follows:
